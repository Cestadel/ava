Product Features

Sturdy dual layer

Composed by leather on both sides, the dual layer of our Shammy enables the players to use both sides to remove the oil efficiently. The dual layer of our Shammy ensures a better product-lifespan.

Power stitching

Our developed stitching technology fortifies the quality of the Shammy’s borders which provides an improved grip.

Non-abrasive

Soft finishing means that your ball is as good as new for a longer time. Say good bye to scratches or marks from your usual fibre cloth or shammy pad

Works on all ball types

Be it Plastic, Urethane, Resin or a Proactive ball, our Shammy offers high oil-absorption and helps you with those extra strikes. Curve the ball as you like!

Personalisation

Colors

We have a range of standard colors to choose from but ofcourse, we give you total control over the choice of the shammy pad color with bulk orders.

Logo

As an optional add-on service, you can add a personalized logo on the Shammy (For your bowling center, your club, for tournaments…etc.)

Cost effective

Our high-end Shammy pads are customizable. Hence, the price of the product too. You have the option to choose from the standard version or from a variety of add-on features. Moreover, our Shammy is washable. Simply hand-wash the Shammy in a sink when needed and let air dry.

Design

In addition to our standard designs, we’ll be glad to provide you the shape or the design of the Shammy you wish for.
